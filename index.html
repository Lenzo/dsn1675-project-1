About

Born & Raised in Toronto

Born in Toronto, to a computer programmer and a cartoonist. I spent my childhood laughing, drawing, pretending, creating and inventing. Being a little kid was awesome.

Being a teen sucked. I dropped out of high school to pursue the school of life. Took the scenic route towards my future for and followed the wind to Ottawa, where I began to grow roots and consider a higher education.

Educated & Employed in Ottawa

College made me understand that I didn’t only like to make things, but that I was good at it.

  1. Graduated Design Studies with honours at Algonquin College 2013
  2. Completed first year of Graphic Design with honours
  3. Currently in my second year of Graphic Design at Algonquin

Now, I have the best job in the world.

I am the in-house Graphic designer at King Empire Entertainment (KEE Recordings inc.), a new record label located in Ottawa. It’s there that I work full time in the summer and part time during school. From outdoor signage to cover art, I do it all. Seeing my work come to life is my greatest satisfaction.
